WELCOME TO CAREER FAIR 2K18

The Annual Career Fair 2k18, Jazz-ify's flagship recruiting event, will be the largest recruiting event of the year and the only Kolkata-wide career fair to be held in the fall.

It is open to companies and students of all disciplines and degree programs.

We invite you to participate in this event to connect with highly talented students from across India.

In past years, the Fall Career Fair has attracted more than 400 companies and 4500 students.

The fair is part of a week-long Career Week in which companies will have numerous opportunities to interact with students.

The 2018 Career Fair will take place on,

Friday, September 29, 2018.

Venue : Saltlake Stadium

Candidate registration will open August 28th, 2018. The 2K18 Career Fair is open to all the students and alumni only.

Please direct any questions regarding the fair to jazz-ifyevent@gmail.com.

*The Fair will be open at 9:30am for freshmen and selected volunteers. Companies are expected to be ready at the Fair by this time. This Early Access Period will allow you to promote your industry, talk about your company, and reach out to future applicants. We kindly ask you refrain from handing out promotional goods during this period to help younger students focus on professional development. You are still welcome to hand out informational brochures or other educational materials. The Fair opens at 10:00am for the general student body.
